Waiting for your love

love is strong, love is true.I’m waiting for it, longing for you.The main smiles, I see your name in the stars but, I can’t read it, it’s still too far.Destiny holds me in the palm of her,I need relief from this hot burning sand.I want to hold you, feel your touch,I’ve waited so long, this is all toomuch.Sweetness abides in the clouds up above,carry me there on the wings of your love.The warmth that comes from the one you love so,in the dead of winter can make flowers grow.I have a treacherous road to travel, before the mysteries of life I unravel.But, you’ll be with me forever, your love in my heart,till the day we meet and from there never part.

Poem by Sofia Grace.

NEW BEAUTY!

Another two hours went by, 23 trains later, she stood up and started to leave. She looked behind her as she heard the familiar sound of a train screeching to a halt. Hope filled her heart once again as it had done 31 trains ago. She stood there and waited, the usual businessman in high collared shirts and blazers with briefcases and young mums with a pushchair and another child hanging onto her hand, the students with a heavy bag of books and the businesswoman in trainers with heels sticking out of her bag, they all bun- dled off the train and headed straight for the exit of the train station.
